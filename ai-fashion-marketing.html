Email Automation

Automated Email
Campaign Flows

Every subscriber gets a personalized journey — triggered by their actions, powered by AI segmentation.

1

Welcome Series — Brand Introduction

3-email sequence over 5 days. Tells your brand story, highlights bestsellers, and offers a 10% first-purchase code. Subject lines A/B tested by AI.

⚡ Trigger: New signup / First visit
2

New Arrivals Announcement

Weekly drop email with AI-curated product blocks based on browsing history. Dynamic images auto-populate from the catalog. Sent at optimal open-time per user.

⚡ Trigger: Every Friday / New collection added
3

Abandoned Cart Recovery

3-step sequence: reminder at 1hr, urgency at 24hr ("Only 2 left!"), discount offer at 48hr. Recovers an avg. 68% of abandoned carts.

⚡ Trigger: Cart abandoned without purchase
4

Post-Purchase + Upsell Flow

Shipping confirmation, delivery update, then a "Complete the look" email with AI-paired accessories and cross-sell recommendations.

⚡ Trigger: Order confirmed
5

Re-Engagement & Win-Back

For subscribers inactive 60+ days — personalized "We miss you" with exclusive discount. Auto-clean list if still unresponsive to keep deliverability high.

⚡ Trigger: 60 days no open or purchase
